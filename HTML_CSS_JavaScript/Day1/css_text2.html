Vocaloids are virtual singers created using vocal synthesis technology. They are designed to mimic human singing voices and can be used to produce music in a variety of styles.
The most notable Vocaloids include Hatsune Miku, Kagamine Rin, and Kagamine Len developed by Crypton Future Media.
Hatsune Miku especially stands out due to her popularity and distinctive voice and recognisable and neutral design.
People recognise her as a virtual idol, fountain of creativity, and a symbol of the Vocaloid phenomenon and the popularity has not waned ever since.
Despite being a digital creation, Hatsune Miku has had a significant impact on music and pop culture worldwide.
